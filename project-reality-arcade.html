Project Reality Arcade is all about balancing PR's realism and fun. This submod intends to make PR less of a hasle to play without eliminating the realistic aspects of the game that made it so special. Features such as vehicle restrictions have been removed entirely, bots can now sprint,special weapons are now unrestricted and spawn times have been reduced to more effectively simulate large scale combined arms warfare.
